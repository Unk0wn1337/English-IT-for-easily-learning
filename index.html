This website has been created to assist first-year students in learning and summarizing IT English. The specific jargon and expressions in the field of Information Technology (IT) can often pose challenges for beginners. Our aim is to provide easily accessible and understandable resources on this platform to help students comprehend IT topics and summarize key concepts. Here, you can find essential IT terminology, vocabulary, and sentence structures that you may need in your IT studies. The content is interactive and easily digestible to aid in preparing for various courses and projects. Additionally, there are opportunities to solve practical examples and challenges, contributing to a deeper understanding of IT English. We hope that this website contributes to the comfortable and effective acquisition of IT English, enabling you to navigate your university studies and progress professionally in the field of information technology. Happy learning!
